Ultimate Bravery, Genshin Impact edition!

Please view on pc... it looks terrible on a phone.

The idea behind ultimate bravery is to receive a random team with random weapons and artifacts, and try to clear as many Abyss floors with as many stars as you can! This only generates one team right now, so you'll have to run it twice. The concept is originally from LoL, you can view it here.

If you don't have a specific artifact main stat, use the next one in the list (same for weapons).

characters
